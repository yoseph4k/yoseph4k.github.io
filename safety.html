Our Safety Philosophy

Killian Construction Company has a primary concern for the safety of its employees. Our goal is to provide a risk-free work environment. We at Killian realize that construction work involves many types of physical hazards, but believe accidents and risks can be greatly reduced if everyone knows and follows safe practices. Our success requires a “team effort…management, supervisors and employees”, with a single goal of safety and health for every individual.
